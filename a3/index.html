COBALT

Cobalt mining is crucial for the production of lithium-ion batteries used in electric vehicles and electronics. The Democratic Republic of Congo (DRC) dominates global cobalt supply, with most cobalt extracted as a byproduct of copper and nickel mining. However, the industry faces ethical concerns, including child labor and environmental damage in artisanal mining operations. Efforts are underway to improve sustainability and traceability in the supply chain. Alternative sources and recycling initiatives are also being explored to reduce dependence on the DRC. Cobalt prices remain volatile, influenced by demand from the EV industry and geopolitical factors. Ongoing research focuses on reducing cobalt usage in batteries while maintaining performance.

Gold

Child labor on a distant planet, where gold is mined for technology, paints a haunting picture of exploitation in the cosmos. In the quest for precious metals to power advanced tech, children find themselves toiling in harsh extraterrestrial conditions. They endure the isolation, inhospitable environments, and long hours, deprived of a normal childhood and education. The demand for gold, essential in interstellar circuitry and devices, drives this tragedy. Addressing this interplanetary crisis necessitates unified efforts by planetary authorities, corporations, and interstellar organizations to establish ethical mining practices, enforce regulations, and liberate child laborers from the cosmic mines, ensuring a more equitable future beyond our world.

Coltan (Tantalum)

Child labor in Coltan (Tantalum) mining on a distant planet for technology reveals a troubling scenario in the cosmos. In the pursuit of vital resources for advanced tech, children are compelled to work in challenging off-world conditions. These young laborers endure harsh environments, extended shifts, and a lack of access to education and a normal childhood. The demand for Coltan, used in interstellar electronics, fuels this crisis. Addressing this interplanetary dilemma calls for collaborative efforts among planetary authorities, corporations, and interstellar organizations to establish ethical mining practices, enforce regulations, and liberate child laborers from the cosmic mines, ensuring a more equitable future beyond our world.
